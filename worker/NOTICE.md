The QQ, Kugou, and NetEase provider-compatibility implementations are derived primarily from the open-source [ESLyric-LyricsSource](https://github.com/Robotxm/ESLyric-LyricsSource) project. The vendored QQ compatibility module in `src/crypto/qrc-eslyric.ts` retains its upstream GPL notice. A copy of GPLv3 is included at `LICENSES/GPL-3.0-only.txt`.

The Worker architecture was informed by [Beautiful Lyrics Reborn](https://github.com/yeahnangua/beautiful-lyrics-reborn) and its upstream [Beautiful Lyrics](https://github.com/surfbryce/beautiful-lyrics) lineage, while parsing and output conversion were rewritten for Spicy Lyrics' native JSON model.
